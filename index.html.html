Regional Ownership
Each CM owns their region — fully

Every local CM is the sole decision-maker for their creator pool. They recruit from within, set the bar, and choose who gets featured. They have direct access to official local game accounts and publish independently — no approval chain, no waiting on SMM. The featuring call is theirs. The growth numbers are theirs.

Content Quality Score

Proposed methodology · to be aligned with Community team

Quality is measured as a composite score — 50% quantitative (platform metrics growth) + 50% qualitative (CM checklist). Both components tracked monthly per creator, then aggregated at region level. The CM owns the assessment for their region.
